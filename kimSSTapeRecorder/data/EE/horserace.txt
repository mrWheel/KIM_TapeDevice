horserace
by Chuck Eaton

Description -
This is an eight lap horse race and you can be the jockey and whip your horse to go faster.  Warning  ...whip the horse too much and he probably poops out.  

The program starts at 0200.

Horse                     Track      Whipping Button
Prince Charming            top              PC
Colorado Cowboy           middle            C
