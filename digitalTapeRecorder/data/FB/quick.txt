QUICK
By Peter Jennings              
Modified by Jim Butterfield

Description -
Here's a program to test your speed of reaction. Press "GO" and the display will blank for a random period of time.
When it lights, hit any numbered button. The number on the display will tell you how quick you were; the smaller the number, the faster your reaction time. You may play repeatedly,

Just press "GO" each time you want a new test.
